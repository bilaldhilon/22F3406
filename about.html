About Service.in

Connecting Quality Service Providers with Customers Since 2024

Our Story

Founded in Faisalabad, Service.in began with a simple mission: to bridge the gap between skilled service professionals and customers who need their expertise. We understand the challenges both service providers and customers face in finding reliable connections.

Starting from CFD Campus, we've grown to become one of the most trusted service platforms in the region, connecting thousands of skilled professionals with customers who need their services.
